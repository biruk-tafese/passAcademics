About us

Vision

Our vision is to create a future where all bright students from low-income backgrounds in Ethiopia have access to international higher education and opportunities, enabling them to break the cycle of poverty and make a positive impact in their communities.

Mission

Our mission is to provide support and resources to students with low financial resources in Ethiopia to help them apply for colleges abroad.
We aim to remove financial barriers by paying for their international exam testing and providing training on how to apply for college.
Additionally, we provide language and other necessary training and resources, such as books and internet access, to help them better prepare for their international tests.

Goals

  • -To increase the number of low-income students in Ethiopia who are able to access international higher education opportunities.
  • -To remove financial barriers that prevent low-income students from pursuing higher education opportunities abroad.
  • -To provide language and other necessary training to enable students to succeed in international exams.
  • -To provide necessary resources such as books and internet access to ensure students are well prepared for their exams.

Objectives

  • -To partner with educational institutions abroad and establish relationships with colleges and universities that offer scholarships and financial aid to low-income students in Ethiopia.
  • -To raise funds and resources to support students' exam fees, language training, and other necessary resources such as books and internet access.
  • -To provide training and mentorship programs to students to prepare them for international exams and the college application process.
  • -To track and monitor students' progress and provide ongoing support to ensure they successfully complete their exams and gain admission to college abroad
